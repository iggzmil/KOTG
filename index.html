Our Story

King of the Grill was born from a love of smoky BBQ and good company. Instead of keeping the flavor locked in a restaurant, we decided to take it on the road – bringing mouth-watering BBQ to backyards, parks, offices, and events all across Sydney. From birthdays to corporate gatherings, our mobile grills fire up fresh, sizzling food that brings people together. At King of the Grill, BBQ isn't just a meal – it's an experience.
